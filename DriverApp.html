THE DELIVERY DRIVER UTILITY APP

The Delivery Driver Utility App helps drivers track patterns in tipping and helps give them better insights into their next offer.

I was able to build its basic functionality in under two months. A fun project, this utility app helped my understanding of backend development and data management . Its core features are the ability to search through customers and streets and the ability to view individual customers and their tipping patterns. I am currently remaking the UI in Figma to make the app UI and UX more streamlined and appealing.
