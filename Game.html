The Tower Defence Game

A game where the goal is to defend your base while also gathering resources to build a stronger defence while waves of enemies spawn.

My first attempt at the game development cycle. I designed the models in Blender, from the character to the enemies, environment like trees and rocks, to the buildings. I then used an OpenGL script to make the grass generate and move based on perlin noise. The enemies spawn in and walk to your base, they start breaking down the gates and their goal is to destroy the village center, but if the player gets too close the enemies then switch targets. The player can gather resources before the next wave so they can construct towers that will shoot at the enemies.
